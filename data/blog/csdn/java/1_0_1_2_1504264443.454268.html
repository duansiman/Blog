接口型设计模式（Interface  patterns）

Interface-oriented patterns address contexts in which you need to define or redefine access to
the methods of a class or group of classes.
 
面向接口的模式（Interface-oriented patterns ）适用于对一个类或一组类中的方法进行定义或重新定义的场景。

You can simplify and strengthen your designs with appropriate application of Java interfaces.
     合理使用java的接口，可以简化并增强我们的设计。
