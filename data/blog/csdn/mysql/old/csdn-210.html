tomcat部署之后 本机访问本地部署的网站打开一个页面非常慢。有时甚至会造成页面空白或直接卡死。

\n

解决方法：是连接池出的问题。改用c3p0连接池。

\n

\n

将数据库连接池改用c3p0连接池。

\n

<bean id="dataSource" class="com.mchange.v2.c3p0.ComboPooledDataSource">\n <property name="driverClass" value="com.mysql.jdbc.Driver">\n </property>\n <property name="jdbcUrl"\n value="jdbc:mysql://localhost:3306/mldn">\n </property>\n <property name="user" value="root"></property>\n <property name="password" value="root"></property>\n <property name="minPoolSize" value="20" />\n <property name="maxPoolSize" value="200" />\n <property name="maxIdleTime" value="30" />\n <property name="acquireIncrement" value="2" />\n <property name="maxStatements" value="200" />\n <property name="initialPoolSize" value="10" />\n <property name="acquireRetryAttempts" value="60" />\n <property name="breakAfterAcquireFailure" value="false" />\n <property name="testConnectionOnCheckout" value="false" />\n <property name="acquireRetryDelay" value="1000" />\n <property name="idleConnectionTestPeriod" value="30"></property>\n </bean>
\n

注意;以下这些语句不能改

\n

<property name="minPoolSize" value="20" />\n <property name="maxPoolSize" value="200" />\n <property name="maxIdleTime" value="30" />\n <property name="acquireIncrement" value="2" />\n <property name="maxStatements" value="200" />\n <property name="initialPoolSize" value="10" />\n <property name="acquireRetryAttempts" value="60" />\n <property name="breakAfterAcquireFailure" value="false" />\n <property name="testConnectionOnCheckout" value="false" />\n <property name="acquireRetryDelay" value="1000" />\n <property name="idleConnectionTestPeriod" value="30"></property>

\n
