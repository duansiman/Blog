\nredis核心配置文件redis.conf配置详解
\n
\nredis支持很多的参数，但是都有默认值
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n启动多个redis
\n
\n方法1
\n
\n启动时指定端口可在一台服务器启动多个redis进程，多个redis实例。
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n方法2:
\n
\n复制redis目录，然后修改redis.conf配置文件中的端口，推荐使用
\n
\n步骤一：
\n
\n拷贝redis目录
\n
\ncp -r redis/ redis6380
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n步骤二：
\n
\n修改redis.conf配置文件
\n
\ncd redis6380/etc
\n
\nvim redis.conf
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n步骤三：
\n
\n启动多个redis服务
\n
\ncd /usr/local/
\n
\n./redis/bin/redis-server ./redis/etc/redis.conf
\n
\n./redis6380/bin/redis-server ./redis6380/etc/redis.conf
\n
\npx aux | grep -i redis
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n
\n步骤四：
\n
\n关闭指定端口号的redis服务
\n
\n./redis/bin/redis-cli shutdown
\n
\n./redis6380/bin/redis-cli shutdown
\n
\n或者
\n
\n./redis/bin/redis-cli -p 6379 shutdown
\n
\n./redis/bin/redis-cli -p 6380 shutdown
\n
