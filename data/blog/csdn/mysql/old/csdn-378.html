一、公共配置
\n
请在三个虚拟机上分别配置此处的配置项。
\n

\n
\n
1. 安装虚拟机
\n
虚拟机操作系统安装CentOS 6.5的x86_64版本，使用NAT网络，并且还要安装vmware-tools，具体安装方法此处不详述。
\n
2. 拷贝mysql cluster
\n
下载以下版本的MySQL-Cluster：
\nhttp://dev.mysql.com/Downloads/MySQL-Cluster-7.3/mysql-cluster-gpl-7.3.4-linux-glibc2.5-x86_64.tar.gz \n

\n

\n
下载得到的压缩包拷贝至虚拟机的/root/Downloads目录，然后在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
cd /root/Downloads
\ntar -xvzf mysql-cluster-gpl-7.3.4-linux-glibc2.5-x86_64.tar.gz
\nmv mysql-cluster-gpl-7.3.4-linux-glibc2.5-x86_64 /usr/local/mysql
\n
\n
3. 关闭安全策略
\n
关闭iptables防火墙（或者打开防火墙的1186、3306端口），在Shell中运行以下命令：
\n
chkconfig --level 35 iptables off
\n
\n
关闭SELinux，在Shell中运行以下命令：
\n
gedit /etc/selinux/config
\n

\n

\n
将config文件中的SELINUX项改为disabled，修改后的config文件的内容如下：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
# This file controls the state of SELinux on the system.
\n# SELINUX= can take one of these three values:
\n#     enforcing - SELinux security policy is enforced.
\n#     permissive - SELinux prints warnings instead of enforcing.
\n#     disabled - No SELinux policy is loaded.
\nSELINUX=disabled
\n# SELINUXTYPE= can take one of these two values:
\n#     targeted - Targeted processes are protected,
\n#     mls - Multi Level Security protection.
\nSELINUXTYPE=targeted
\n
最后重启系统
\n
\n
\n
二、配置管理结点（192.168.124.141）
\n
1. 配置config.ini配置文件
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
\n
mkdir /var/lib/mysql-cluster
\n
cd /var/lib/mysql-cluster
\n
gedit config.ini
\n
\n
\n
配置文件config.ini内容如下：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
[ndbd default]
\nNoOfReplicas=2
\nDataMemory=80M
\nIndexMemory=18M
\n
\n[ndb_mgmd]
\nNodeId=1
\nhostname=192.168.124.141
\ndatadir=/var/lib/mysql-cluster
\n
\n[ndbd]
\nNodeId=2
\nhostname=192.168.124.142
\ndatadir=/usr/local/mysql/data
\n
\n
[ndbd]
\nNodeId=3
\nhostname=192.168.124.143
\ndatadir=/usr/local/mysql/data
\n
\n
[mysqld]
\nNodeId=4
\nhostname=192.168.124.144
\n
\n[mysqld]
\nNodeId=5
\nhostname=192.168.124.145
\n
\n
2. 安装管理结点
\n
安装管理节点，不需要mysqld二进制文件，只需要MySQL Cluster服务端程序(ndb_mgmd)和监听客户端程序(ndb_mgm)。在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
cp /usr/local/mysql/bin/ndb_mgm* /usr/local/bin
\ncd /usr/local/bin
\nchmod +x ndb_mgm*
\n
\n
三、配置数据结点（192.168.124.142、192.168.124.143）
\n
1. 添加mysql组和用户
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
groupadd mysql
\nuseradd -g mysql mysql
\n

\n
\n
\n
2. 配置my.cnf配置文件
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
gedit /etc/my.cnf
\n
\n
配置文件my.cnf的内容如下：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
[mysqld]
\nbasedir=/usr/local/mysql
\ndatadir=/usr/local/mysql/data
\nsocket=/usr/local/mysql/sock/mysql.sock
\nuser=mysql
\n# Disabling symbolic-links is recommended to prevent assorted security risks
\nsymbolic-links=0
\n
\n[mysqld_safe]
\nlog-error=/var/log/mysqld.log
\npid-file=/var/run/mysqld/mysqld.pid
\n
\n[mysql_cluster]
\nndb-connectstring=192.168.124.141
\n

\n
\n
\n
3. 创建系统数据库
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
cd /usr/local/mysql
\nmkdir sock
\nscripts/mysql_install_db --user=mysql --basedir=/usr/local/mysql --datadir=/usr/local/mysql/data
\n
\n
4. 设置数据目录
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
chown -R root .
\nchown -R mysql.mysql /usr/local/mysql/data
\nchown -R mysql.mysql /usr/local/mysql/sock
\nchgrp -R mysql .
\n

\n
\n
\n
5. 配置MySQL服务
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
cp support-files/mysql.server /etc/rc.d/init.d/
\nchmod +x /etc/rc.d/init.d/mysql.server
\nchkconfig --add mysql.server
\n

\n
\n
\n

五、Cluster环境启动
\n
注意启动顺序：首先是管理节点，然后是数据节点，最后是SQL节点。
\n
1. 启动管理结点
\n
在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
ndb_mgmd -f /var/lib/mysql-cluster/config.ini
\n

\n
\n
\n
还可以使用ndb_mgm来监听客户端，如下：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
ndb_mgm
\n

\n
\n
\n
2. 启动数据结点
\n
首次启动，则需要添加--initial参数，以便进行NDB节点的初始化工作。在以后的启动过程中，则是不能添加该参数的，否则ndbd程序会清除在之前建立的所有用于恢复的数据文件和日志文件。
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
/usr/local/mysql/bin/ndbd --initial
\n

\n
\n
\n
如果不是首次启动，则执行下面的命令。
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
/usr/local/mysql/bin/ndbd
\n

\n
\n
\n
3. 启动SQL结点
\n
若MySQL服务没有运行，则在shell中运行以下命令：
\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n
/usr/local/mysql/bin/mysqld_safe --user=mysql &
\n

\n
\n
\n
