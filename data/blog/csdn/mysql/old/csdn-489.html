3.6.1 列的最大值
\n“最大的物品号是多少？”

\n\n\n\n
SELECT MAX(article) AS article FROM shop;\n+---------+\n| article |\n+---------+\n| 4 |\n+---------+
\n\n

3.6.2 拥有某列最大值的行

\n\n

任务：找出最贵商品的编号，经销商和价格

\n\n

用一个子查询很容易做到：

\n\n\n\n
SELECT article, dealer, price\nFROM shop\nWHERE price=(SELECT MAX(price) FROM shop);\n+---------+--------+-------+\n| article | dealer | price |\n+---------+--------+-------+\n| 0004 | D | 19.95 |\n+---------+--------+-------+
\n\n

另一个解决方案是使用LEFT JOIN或者按照价格降序排序所有行，然后使用MySQL特定的LIMIT子句只取第一行：

\n\n\n\n
SELECT s1.article, s1.dealer, s1.price\nFROM shop s1\nLEFT JOIN shop s2 ON s1.price < s2.price\nWHERE s2.article IS NULL;\nSELECT article, dealer, price\nFROM shop\nORDER BY price DESC\nLIMIT 1;
\n\n

注意：如果有多个最贵的物品，假设每个都是19.95，那么LIMIT只会取其中的一个.

\n\n

3.6.3 按组找出列的最大值
\n任务：找出每项物品的最高价格.

\n\n\n\n
SELECT article, MAX(price) AS price\nFROM shop\nGROUP BY article;\n+---------+-------+\n| article | price |\n+---------+-------+\n| 0001 | 3.99 |\n| 0002 | 10.99 |\n| 0003 | 1.69 |\n| 0004 | 19.95 |\n+---------+-------+
\n\n

3.6.4 拥有某个字段的组间最大值的行

\n\n

任务：对每项物品，找出最贵价格的经销商.

\n\n

这个问题可用这样一个子查询来解决：

\n\n\n\n
SELECT article, dealer, price\nFROM shop s1\nWHERE price=(SELECT MAX(s2.price)\nFROM shop s2\nWHERE s1.article = s2.article);\n+---------+--------+-------+\n| article | dealer | price |\n+---------+--------+-------+\n| 0001 | B | 3.99 |\n| 0002 | A | 10.99 |\n| 0003 | C | 1.69 |\n| 0004 | D | 19.95 |\n+---------+--------+-------+
\n\n

上面的例子使用了一个相互关联的子查询，这种效率比较低（查看 Section 13.2.10.7,“Correlated Subqueries” ）. 其他可能的解决方案在From子句中使用了一个不相关的子查询或LEFT JOIN.

\n\n

不相关的子查询：

\n\n\n\n
SELECT s1.article, dealer, s1.price\nFROM shop s1\nJOIN (\nSELECT article, MAX(price) AS price\nFROM shop\nGROUP BY article) AS s2\nON s1.article = s2.article AND s1.price = s2.price;
\n\n

LEFT JOIN:

\n\n\n\n
SELECT s1.article, s1.dealer, s1.price\nFROM shop s1\nLEFT JOIN shop s2 ON s1.article = s2.article AND s1.price < s2.price\nWHERE s2.article IS NULL;
\n\n

LEFT JOIN是基于s1.price达到最大值有效的，那时s2.price不会有更大的值并且s2那一行将为NULL. 查看 Section 13.2.9.2, “JOIN
\nSyntax”.
