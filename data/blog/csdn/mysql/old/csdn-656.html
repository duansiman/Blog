\n\n

问题描述如下：我们在eclipse中向MySQL中插入数据

\n\n\n\n
statement.executeUpdate("INSERT INTO word VALUES （'鲜花'，'2')");
\n\n

然而我们发现
\n
\n解决方法：我们为mysql数据库的连接字符串设置编码方式
\n将原先的代码

\n\n\n\n
Connection c =DriverManager.getConnection("jdbc:mysql://localhost:3306/words","root","");
\n\n

修改为

\n\n\n\n
Connection c\n =DriverManager.getConnection("jdbc:mysql://localhost:3306/words?characterEncoding=utf8","root","");
